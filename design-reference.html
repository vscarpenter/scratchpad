1e V4 · Soft glass a modern native-feeling app — depth, blur, rounded

Panels float as frosted glass cards over a faint tinted wash. The serif gives way to the system sans; the indigo lifts to a brighter #4E5FD8. Pills, squircles and soft shadows everywhere; the trust message becomes a status chip. Closest to a macOS/iOS companion app.
